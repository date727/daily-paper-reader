## 摘要
我们研究了在 $ d = 4 - ε $ 维的阿贝尔-希格斯模型中，在大外部电荷下的带尖点的Wilson线算符。通过在 $ Q \to \infty $、$ ε\to 0 $ 的双重缩放极限下保持 $ Qε $ 固定，我们发展了一种半经典框架，从而实现超越固定阶微扰理论的解析控制。我们计算了任意电荷下的尖点异常维数，将其在规范耦合的次次主导项阶展开，同时将标量自相互作用重新求和到所有阶。我们的结果在微扰与大电荷区域之间进行插值，能够访问固定阶微扰理论无法覆盖的领域。作为应用，我们提供了多种缺陷CFT可观测量的新预测，包括描述超导相变的Mandelstam-Schwinger修正两点函数。

## Abstract
We study cusped Wilson line operators in the Abelian Higgs model in $ d = 4 - ε$ at large external charges. Using a double-scaling limit $ Q \to \infty $, $ ε\to 0 $ with $ Qε$ fixed, we develop a semiclassical framework that provides analytic control beyond fixed-order perturbation theory. We compute the cusp anomalous dimension for arbitrary charges up to next-to-next-to-leading order in the gauge coupling, while resumming scalar self-interactions to all orders. Our results interpolate between perturbative and large-charge regimes, accessing domains that are invisible in fixed-order perturbation theory. As an application, we provide new predictions for various defect CFT observables, including the Mandelstam-Schwinger-dressed two-point function characterizing the superconducting phase transition.

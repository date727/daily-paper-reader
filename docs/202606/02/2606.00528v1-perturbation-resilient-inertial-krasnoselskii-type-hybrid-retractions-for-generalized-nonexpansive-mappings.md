## 摘要
设$E$是一个一致光滑且一致凸的实Banach空间。我们研究满足NST条件且具有扰动鲁棒性的惯性Krasnosel'skii型混合重映射方案，适用于一个可数的广义非扩张映射族$Γ$。主要结果表明，当将精确的φ-Fejér递减条件替换为可求和扰动版本时，强收敛性得以保持。在生成的收缩集的适当结构假设下，我们证明所得到的序列强收敛于晴朗的广义非扩张重缩映射$R_{F(Γ)}v_0$。这为现有的无误差混合重映射方法提供了稳定性改进，并为处理计算不精确性（如近似投影和不精确算子计算）提供了框架。我们还讨论了该方法的Bregman--Fejér解释，并在一般Bregman设定所需的额外结构假设下，提出了Bregman投影的类比形式。

## Abstract
Let $E$ be a uniformly smooth and uniformly convex real Banach space. We study perturbation-resilient inertial Krasnosel'skii-type hybrid retraction schemes for a countable family of generalized nonexpansive mappings satisfying the NST-condition with a family $Γ$. The main result shows that strong convergence is preserved when the exact $φ$-Fejér decrease condition is replaced by a summably perturbed version. Under suitable structural assumptions on the generated shrinking sets, we prove that the resulting sequence converges strongly to the sunny generalized nonexpansive retraction $R_{F(Γ)}v_0$. This provides a stability refinement of existing error-free hybrid retraction methods and gives a framework for treating computational inaccuracies such as approximate projections and inexact operator evaluations. We also discuss a Bregman--Fejér interpretation of the method and formulate a Bregman--projection analogue under the additional structural assumptions required in the general Bregman setting.
